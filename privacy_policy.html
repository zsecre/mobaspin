Privacy Policy — Mobaspin

Effective date: September 30, 2025 · Contact: artbeastdm@gmail.com

This Privacy Policy explains how Mobaspin (“we”, “us”, “our”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Mobaspin mobile application. This is a short, plain-language policy suitable for display on-app store listings or a website.

1. Information we collect

Information you provide:

  • MLBB ID and ZONE ID: You enter these to use the service so we can identify you and deliver prizes. We store MLBB ID as the primary user key in our database.
  • Support messages: If you contact us we may retain the message and your email/contact details to respond.

Information automatically collected:

  • Device & technical data (device model, OS version, app version, basic diagnostics).
  • Usage data such as when you spin and which screens you visit (for analytics and abuse detection).
  • Advertising identifier (Google Advertising ID or platform equivalent) used by ad SDKs for ad serving and measurement.

2. How we use your information

  • Save your account (MLBB ID and ZONE ID) and manage spins and prizes.
  • Store spin history, spin chance and totals so features work correctly.
  • Display ads and measure ad performance (via third-party ad networks).
  • Detect and prevent fraud and abuse; troubleshoot and improve the app.

3. Where data is stored

Your user data (MLBB ID, ZONE ID, spin history, spin_chance, totals) is stored in our Firebase Realtime Database. Third-party providers (for example ad networks or analytics) may also collect and store data under their own policies.

4. Data retention

We retain records necessary to operate the service (your user node and a limited spin history). You can request deletion of your data — see the Contact section below. We may retain anonymized aggregate data indefinitely.

5. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data in transit and at rest (HTTPS, access controls). No storage or transmission method is 100% secure; absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

6. Ads & third-party services

We use Google AdMob (and possibly other ad networks). These services may collect identifiers and technical signals for ad serving and analytics. We do not control their data practices — consult the third party’s privacy policy for details.

7. Your rights & choices

  • Access / Correction / Deletion: You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information. Provide your MLBB ID when you contact us.
  • Ads: You can opt out of personalized ads via your device ad settings (Android Advertising ID settings).

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make material changes we will update the “Effective date” above and may notify you in the app or via the store listing.

9. Contact

If you have questions or wish to make a data request, contact us at: artbeastdm@gmail.com

Quick summary: Mobaspin stores MLBB ID and ZONE ID and basic spin data to operate the app, uses Firebase for storage and AdMob for advertising, does not sell personal data, and allows you to request deletion. © 2025 Mobaspin.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Mobaspin?

Mobaspin is a free-to-play web app where you can win in-game currency for Mobile Legends and Honor of Kings.

How do I get spins?

You get 3 free spins per day for each game. Just log in with your game ID to claim them.

Is this website safe?

Yes, Mobaspin is safe to use. We only require your game ID to send you the prizes you win.

How long will it take to receive the prize I won?

Prizes are typically processed within 1 to 24 hours. You will receive the prize directly in your in-game mail.
